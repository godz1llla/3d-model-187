Korkyt Ata Kyzylorda University 
Kyzylorda University named after Korkyt Ata is a higher education institution in Kyzylorda, Kazakhstan. It was founded in 1937. In 6 institutes of the university at 24 departments, specialists will be trained on 66 educational programmes of bachelor's degree, 34 programmes of master's degree, 13 educational programmes of doctoral studies (PhD) within the following directions: pedagogical, humanities, social, natural sciences, art, business and management, law, engineering and technology, agriculture. At the University about 550 teaching staff give lectures for more than 10 thousand students. There are also 5 scientific centres and military departments, 95 branches of departments at industrial enterprises.

Task. The main building of the Korkyt Ata Kyzylorda University consists of 5 floors and 15 classrooms on each floor. Each layer forms a rectangle with certain dimensions. 
1. If the length of the corridor on each floor is 50 meters and the width is 10 meters, then what is the total area of the corridors on all floors?
2. If each floor is 75 square meters, what is the total area occupied by lecture halls on all floors?
3. If all classrooms on each floor have 10 tables measuring 1 meter by 0.5 meters, then what is the total area of tables on all floors?
Note:all rooms have vertical corners and each floor has the same size.

Solution:
1.The total area of corridor coverage on all floors is equal to the product of the area of one corridor by the number of floors. The area of one corridor is equal to the product of its length and width: 50 m?10 m = 500 m2.
Thus, the total area of the corridors on all floorsis 500 m2 ? 5 =2500 m2.
2. The total area of lecture halls on all floors is equal to the area of one of them multiplied by the number of all lecture halls. The area of one is 75 square meters. Thus, the total area of lecture halls on all floors will be75?5?15=5625 m2.
3. The area of one table surface:1?0,5=0,5 m2
The surface area of one table in the auditorium:0,5 ?10=5 m2
    Tablesurfaceareaonallfloors:5?5=25 m2. This is how the total area of all tables on all floors is calculated.

Answer: area of one corridor is 500 m2; the total area of lecture halls is 5625 m2; the surface area of tables is 25 m2.
