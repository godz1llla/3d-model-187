[TITLE]
Syr Ana Monument
[DESCRIPTION]
The Syr Ana Monument is a complex erected in 2001 on the eve of the 20th anniversary of the country's independence as a gift for the Independence Day celebration. From a distance the structure brings to mind the image of a beautiful bride. The height of the monument is 39 meters. From the observation deck at a height of 32 meters there is a view of the entire city. The base structure of the monument combines a cone with a hexagonal prism. The dome features triangles or a right pyramid. The top is made to resemble a saukele headdress that highlights the image of a Kazakh woman. The tower top in the image of the saukele is completed with a detail in the shape of scattered rice. As this project aims to glorify our Syr Ana, it has become an architectural landmark that embodies the grandeur of the nation.
