[TITLE]
"Batyrs of Syr Region" Monument
[DESCRIPTION]
The monument depicting four batyrs stands 5.5 m high and weighs 5 tons. The monument is cast in bronze, with the pedestal cladded in granite. Authors: A. Kenenbaev, Zh. Ismagulov. It is recommended for the preliminary list of historical and cultural heritage sites. Located on Konaev Street in Kyzylorda.
