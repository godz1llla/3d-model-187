[TITLE]
Anaga Tagzim Center
[DESCRIPTION]
"Anaga Tagzim" Center is a newly built cultural and social facility of national and modern type in the city center. The Anaga Tagzim Center is a blessed Shanyrak, where mothers and brides of all "Syr Eli" gather. The starting time of the center construction is planned to February, 2023. The building area is 1379.3 m2.
