[TITLE]
Wedding Palace
[DESCRIPTION]
The "Wedding Palace" center was built and put into operation in 2023. The building consists of two floors. The first floor has a waiting room, elevator hall, wardrobe, rooms for administration and staff. The second floor has a small hall, large halls, wedding rooms, guest halls.
