[TITLE]
Madina Eralieva House of Culture
[DESCRIPTION]
The Madina Eralieva House of Culture is located on Kashaubaev Street in Titov village, Kyzylorda. In accordance with the 2003 Decree of the Government of the Republic of Kazakhstan No. 1021, the former "Bumazhnik" palace of culture was renamed after the Honored Artist of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Madina Eralieva. It was built according to a standard design.
