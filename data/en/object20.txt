[TITLE]
Sabira Maykanova House of Culture
[DESCRIPTION]
The house of culture was built to a standard design in 1989. In March 1996, by decision of the Syrdarya district akimat and the regional onomastic commission, it was named after USSR People's Artist S. Maykanova. It is located on Sh. Yesenov Street in the village of Tasboget.
[TASK]

1. Find the area of the given wall of the house of culture named after S. Maykanova.
2. Find the area of the windows on this wall.
