[TITLE]
Kyzylorda Regional Art Gallery
[DESCRIPTION]
On the first floor of the building, there are 3 exhibition halls displaying over 260 paintings by artists such as A. Kasteev, G. Khludov, G. Ismailov, A. Galymbaeva, S. Senchikhin, K. Kozhykov, O. Mamakova, and others. Additionally, there are 6 workshops on the second floor for artists' creative work.
[TASK]
Task 16. The building is divided into two parts. One part contains 3 identical exhibition halls, and the other part is divided into 6 identical auditoriums. Find the total internal area of the building if the area of one hall is 80 m² and the area of one auditorium is 20 m².
[ANSWER]
360 m²
